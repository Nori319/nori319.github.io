PageSentinel Privacy Policy

Enacted: June 9, 2025

Last Updated: August 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as “Policy”) sets forth the rules on how the individual developer (hereinafter “Developer”) of the smartphone app PageSentinel (hereinafter “App”) handles user data with respect to privacy protection. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Article 1 (Information Collected)

  1. User Input Data
    URLs to be monitored, reload intervals, CSS selectors, and other information entered or configured in the App.
  2. In-App Settings
    User-selected display settings, Anti-Flicker level, scroll delays, and other UI/UX related settings.
  3. Device Metadata
    OS version, device type (iPhone/iPad), and other technical information required for operation and optimization. No UUIDs or personally identifying information are collected.

Article 2 (Purpose of Use)

  • Monitoring and reloading of user-specified web pages
  • Automatic reload control and anti-flicker prevention
  • Persisting and restoring in-app settings
  • Analysis of anonymized technical data for bug fixes and feature improvements

Article 3 (Prohibition of Third-Party Provision)

The Developer will not provide collected information to any third party without the user’s consent, except as required by law.

Article 4 (Cookies and Web Technologies)

The App uses WebView to display web pages. Any cookies or localStorage use is subject to the policies of the displayed website and is not collected by the App itself.

Article 5 (Data Retention)

User input and settings remain stored locally on the device until the App is uninstalled. No external servers are used to store such data.

Article 6 (Data Security)

  • App data is stored in local storage inaccessible to other apps under normal conditions.
  • Data migration is implemented to prevent data corruption during updates.

Article 7 (User Rights)

  • Delete or modify settings within the App
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App

Article 8 (Disclaimer)

The Developer provides the App “as is” without warranties of fitness for a particular purpose or uninterrupted operation. Functionality may be affected by changes in target websites or JavaScript specifications.

Article 9 (Changes to the Policy)

The Developer may update this Policy when deemed necessary. Updates will take effect once published in the App or on the Developer’s website.
